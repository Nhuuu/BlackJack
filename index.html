    Goal

    Beat the dealer by getting a hand as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21. A "blackjack" occurs when you get one ace and one 10 point card.

    Card Values

    Queens, Kings, and Jacks are worth 10, pip cards are worth their face value. Aces can be worth either 11 or 1. In this game, Aces have a default value of 11 unless you go over 21 on your hand.

    Gameplay

    The dealer will give 2 cards to themself and 2 cards to you. The dealer's second card will be played face down. You can choose to either hit (receive more cards) or stand (keep your cards as is). You can hit as many times as you choose so long as your deck is under 21.

    Betting

    Bets must be placed prior to playing. After receiving your first 2 cards, you can choose to double down (double your original bet). After your next move, you can no longer choose to double down.

    Splitting Pairs

    If you receive 2 cards of the same face value, you may choose to split your cards and play each hand separately. At this table, you can only split once. After you split, one new card will be dealt to each hand then you can choose to either hit or stand for each hand accordingly.

    Payout

    Payout is 1:1 unless you get a blackjack. For a blackjack, payout is 3:2.
